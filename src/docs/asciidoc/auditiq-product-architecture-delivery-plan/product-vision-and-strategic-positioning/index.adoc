== Product Vision and Strategic Positioning

=== Vision Statement

Make every financial institution always exam-ready by providing a unified audit workflow platform that combines human expertise with AI-assisted efficiency, immutable evidence management, and confidence-scored transparency — so that compliance teams can focus on judgment, not data entry.

=== Strategic Positioning

AuditIQ is positioned as:

* *Not an AI product* — it is a governance and workflow platform that uses AI as an accelerant.
* *Additive, not replacement* — it extends RiskExec’s existing compliance platform, increasing deal size by 20–30%.
* *Examiner-friendly* — designed to produce the evidence packages and audit trails that regulators want to see.
* *AI opt-out, not opt-in* — per Board directive, AI capabilities are available by default but can be fully disabled. (Source: 2026_02.10 - CEO Letter)
