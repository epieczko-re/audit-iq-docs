==== Primary Competitor Strategic Assessment

* *Defensibility:* MEDIUM-HIGH. Deep enterprise relationships and switching costs, but aging architecture and bolt-on AI.
* *AI strategy:* Introduced AI features via partnerships (not native) in September 2025. Cannot easily retrofit governance-first AI controls.
* *Weaknesses:* QC-only (not three lines of defense), documentation quality criticized, limited OCR/document extraction capabilities.
* *Pricing vulnerability:* Per-pack pricing without AI features means customers pay more for less intelligence. AuditIQ can include AI governance controls at competitive pricing and win on value.
