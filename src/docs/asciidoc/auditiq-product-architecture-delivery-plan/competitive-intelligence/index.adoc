== 4. Competitive Intelligence

=== 4.1 ACES Quality Management (Primary Competitor)

* *Market position:* ~141 customers; avg ACV $135K; banks avg $167K, servicers $158K. (Source: ACES Pricing Model.txt)
* *Pricing:* Tiered subscription: $3.3K/month (100 audits, $28/excess) to $27.5K/month (5,000 audits, $4/excess). Enterprise HMDA: $4.2K/month for 3,000 audits. (Source: ACES Pricing Model.txt)
* *Defensibility:* MEDIUM-HIGH. Deep enterprise relationships and switching costs, but aging architecture and bolt-on AI. (Source: ACES_Competitive Analysis_vF.pdf)
* *AI strategy:* Introduced AI features via partnerships (not native) in September 2025. Cannot easily retrofit governance-first AI controls.
* *Weaknesses:* QC-only (not three lines of defense), documentation quality criticized, limited OCR/document extraction capabilities.
