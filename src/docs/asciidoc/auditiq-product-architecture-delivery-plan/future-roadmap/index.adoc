== 16. Future Roadmap and Platform Leverage

=== 16.1 Carry-Forward from v0.1: Platform Expansion Vision

*AuditIQ is positioned as the foundational audit automation layer.* Future modules leverage the pack engine, immutable audit trail, and scoring framework:

=== 16.2 Phase 2: Expansion (Q3 2026, Jul–Sep)

*Conversational Copilot*

* Natural language interface to audit data
* Example: ``Show me all critical findings in commercial loans over $1M''
* Powered by RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) over audit trail + findings
* Use case: Manager ``what-if'' analysis without scripting

*Predictive Validation Rules*

* ML models that learn from pack rule outcomes + reviewer corrections
* Example: ``Loan docs missing interest rate calc sheet → 85% likelihood of missing disclosure''
* Enables proactive finding prediction on new loans
* Calibration study required (Phase 1b baseline → Phase 2 retraining)

*Adaptive Learning*

* Feedback loops: Reviewer decisions → confidence recalibration
* Example: If matcher suggests mapping with 0.72 confidence, but reviewer corrects 20% of the time, lower threshold to 0.60
* Telemetry dashboard showing real-time drift in model accuracy per pack per customer
* Automated alerts if override rate >15% (indicate model degradation)

*Multi-Pack Audit Workflows* - Chain multiple packs in single audit (e.g., ``HMDA + CRA + Pricing packs'') - Findings merge, confidence scoring aggregates across packs - Dependency tracking (e.g., ``HMDA finding prerequisites CRA data'')

=== 16.3 Phase 3 & Beyond: Platform Ecosystem (Q4 2026+)

*Pack Marketplace*

* Community-driven pack registry (GitHub-based or proprietary platform)
* Vendor packs from compliance consultants, audit firms
* Rating & review system for pack quality
* Monetization: Revenue share on vendor packs (80/20 or 70/30 split TBD)
* Example packs: ECOA/FHA audits, FCRA disputes, loan matrix validation, LIBOR remediation

*Policy Management Module*

* Spin-off product: Audit policies, rule versioning, change tracking
* Regulators demand traceability of ``which rule version was in effect when''
* Feature: Policy library (CRA, ECOA, HMDA, fair lending) + versioning + audit trail
* Integration: Packs reference policies; policy changes auto-trigger pack version updates

*Audit Data Warehouse & BI*

* Customer data lake: Aggregated audit results, trends, metrics
* Example: ``What % of commercial loan audits failed CRA pricing component in 2025?''
* BI dashboards: Risk heat maps, audit metrics by region, examiner prep reports
* Data residency & encryption respected; opt-in for analytics

*Third-Party Integrations* - LMS (Loan Management System): Pre-populate loan data into audit - Regulatory reporting: Export findings to FDIC/OCC submission tools - Workflow engines: Zapier, Make, RPA tools for audit orchestration - API-first strategy ensures extensibility

*Vertical Expansion Beyond Lending* - Insurance audits: Policy compliance, claims validation - Credit unions: Regulatory exam preparation - Fund management: Compliance audits, valuation verification - Vertical teams (Q4+) develop packs; core platform scales

=== 16.4 Strategic Rationale

*Network effects:* Pack marketplace + community contributions accelerate audit coverage and reduce pack development cost for platform.

*Platform stickiness:* Copilot + predictive rules + policy integration make AuditIQ harder to replace once adopted.

*Data moat:* Audit data warehouse becomes increasingly valuable as customer base grows; enables predictive models that smaller competitors cannot match.
