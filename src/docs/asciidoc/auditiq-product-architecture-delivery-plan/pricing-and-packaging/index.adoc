=== 13.2 RiskExec Baseline & AuditIQ Uplift Strategy

*RiskExec current market baseline:*

* ACV: ~$40,000/year for core risk analytics platform
* Modules: Enterprise risk dashboard, regulatory reporting, model governance
* Growth: Existing customer base; limited new module uptake

*AuditIQ positioning & uplift target:*

* Expected ACV uplift: *20–30% of RiskExec base* for customers adopting audit automation
* Rationale: Reduces audit labor cost by ~40% per engagement; enables higher audit frequency
* Target: $8,000–$12,000/year add-on to RiskExec contracts (or standalone for new audit-first prospects)
