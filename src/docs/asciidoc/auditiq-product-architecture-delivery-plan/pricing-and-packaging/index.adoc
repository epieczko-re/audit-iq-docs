=== RiskExec Baseline & AuditIQ Uplift Strategy

*RiskExec current market baseline:*

* ACV: ~$40,000/year for core risk analytics platform
* Modules: Enterprise risk dashboard, regulatory reporting, model governance
* Growth: Existing customer base; limited new module uptake

*AuditIQ dual positioning:*

* *RiskExec add-on:* 20–30% ACV uplift for existing customers adopting audit automation → target $8K–$12K/yr for single-pack entry, scaling to $50K–$120K/yr for multi-pack enterprise
* *Standalone product:* For audit-first prospects (e.g., incumbent displacement), AuditIQ competes directly at $40K–$150K ACV with full pack portfolio
* *Rationale:* Reduces audit labor cost by ~40% per engagement; enables higher audit frequency; consolidates 3 lines of defense into one system

=== Pricing Model

*DECISION PROPOSED (D6):* Platform + Pack subscription model, mirroring the primary competitor's tiered structure that the market understands, with AI governance controls included as a competitive differentiator.

==== Pricing Architecture

AuditIQ pricing consists of three components:

[arabic]
. *Platform access fee* (required) — covers core workflow, audit trail, evidence management, exam-ready bundles, dashboards, RBAC, and SSO
. *Pack subscriptions* (per pack, tiered by monthly audit volume) — each regulatory pack priced independently with volume tiers
. *Add-ons* (optional) — AI Copilot, custom pack development, professional services

This mirrors the primary competitor's proven model while adding value through included AI governance features.

=== Contract Terms

[cols="30%,70%",options="header"]
|===
|Term |Policy

|*Contract length*
|Annual contracts required. Multi-year (2–3 year) contracts receive 5% / 10% additional discount.

|*Billing*
|Monthly billing against annual commitment. Excess audit fees invoiced monthly in arrears.

|*Annual true-up*
|If actual audit volume exceeds tier for 3+ consecutive months, customer is moved to next tier at renewal (or mid-term with mutual agreement).

|*Cancellation*
|90-day written notice. Data export provided at no charge. Audit trail data retained for 7 years per regulatory requirement.

|*Trial*
|30-day free trial: Essentials platform + HMDA pack, limited to 50 audits, 3 users. No credit card required. Trial data migrates to paid account.
|===

=== Pricing Exclusions

The following are *not* included in AuditIQ pricing and are procured separately:

* Data licensing (HMDA LAR data, regulatory feeds) — customer responsibility
* Regulatory audit/compliance consulting services — partner referral model
* White-label packs — not in scope for v1.0; roadmap item for pack marketplace
* On-premises deployment — cloud-only SaaS for Phase 1; on-prem evaluation in Phase 3 based on demand
